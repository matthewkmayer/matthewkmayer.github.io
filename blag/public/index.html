About

Matthew Mayer has been professionally building software since 2006. He’s earned a living writing C#, Java, Golang and Ruby. Since 2014 he’s focused on cloud native solutions on Amazon Web Services, including achieving the AWS Solutions Architect Professional level certification. Matthew was one of the founding maintainers of Rusoto, the community AWS SDK for Rust. When not typing on a computer, he can be found touring North America on his motorcycle or sidecar rig.
